A note from the dev

V10. The final chapter.

What started as a small experiment with Gemini AI turned into something I never expected—an actual platform people used.

V10 wasn't planned to be the last. But some things end at the right time. I'm proud of every janky commit, every midnight hotfix, every README rewrite. It was worth it.

Mist.AI won’t disappear—V10 is a milestone, not a shutdown. The project will live on as a foundation for what comes next.

Building Mist.AI was a wild ride. There were moments of pure frustration, but also moments of unexpected joy when something finally clicked or when users shared how much they loved the app.

Along the way, I learned more about backend systems, LLM pipelines, frontend architecture, and real-world reliability than any class could teach.

To everyone who used MistAI — thank you. Genuinely.

Kristian Cook, 2024-2026
